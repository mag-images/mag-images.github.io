aah Instagram Story Generator

A simple tool to generate an Instagram story image for aAh Magazine. Simply fill in the details of the article below and then a story image will be produced

1. Type the headline of the article

Six or seven lines maximum, ideally less


2. Choose an image for the story

Landscape, size 1080 by 1920 pixels if possible. Images of any other aspect ratio will be squashed to fit, which doesn't look nice

 

3. Pick the colour scheme

Most posts should be pink, or use blue or orange for a bit of variety


For posts about The Energy Issue, use one of these colours

4. Choose a style for the story

More styles are coming soon once the sections' branding has been developed

5. Decide on a font size



Generally stick with medium, but you can make it bigger or smaller if it would look better.

6. Once everything's finished, click Download and then add it to the Instagram
